CHILDREN'S HOME

Tsunami Relief |Children's Home 		 

   

India is an ancient civilization rich with traditions and history dating back thousands of years. But since India's independence from England a half century ago, it has become the world's largest democracy governing one sixth of our planets population. India's population sailed past the one billion mark sometime August the nineteenth, nineteen ninety-nine. At present projections India will exceed 1.5 billion people by two thousand forty-five, sliding into first place ahead of China.

Explosive growth presents obvious challenges in the areas of health, nutrition, education, public services, and employment, not to mention the environment. Seventy five percent of their poor and impoverished three hundred and fifty million struggle in some seven hundred thousand rural villages. Owing to their rural poverty, more than 40% of India's entire population is illiterate, with the women and children particularly and severely affected.
